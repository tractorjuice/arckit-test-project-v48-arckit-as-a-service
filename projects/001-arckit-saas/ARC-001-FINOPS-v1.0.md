## 1. Why FinOps Matters Here

The whole commercial model depends on knowing the per-tenant cost-to-serve. Without it:

- The free-tier promise (Principle 1; BR-001) is unverified.
- The cross-subsidy break-even target (BR-005) is un-trackable.
- Quota tuning (ADR-008) is guesswork.
- Provider-lock-in arguments are anecdotal.

This strategy operationalises the FinOps Foundation **inform → optimise → operate** cycle for ArcKit.

---

## 2. Cost Telemetry Architecture

- Every infrastructure resource tagged `tenant_id` or `cell_id` (and `tier`, `purpose`).
- AI calls accounted per tenant via the AIAdaptor (ADR-004).
- Storage cost allocated per tenant via prefix metering (ADR-002).
- Identity cost allocated per tenant (ADR-003).
- Observability cost allocated per cell (cardinality control; ADR-005).
- Cell-level shared costs (DB instance, K8s control plane) allocated to tenants pro-rata of consumption signals (storage + AI tokens + API calls).

Outputs: per-tenant unit economics report (free / paid SME / enterprise / sovereign), per-cell cost report, per-feature (e.g., AI generation) cost report.
