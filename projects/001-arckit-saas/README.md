## Scope

This project covers the **managed multi-tenant SaaS** offering of ArcKit as a Service for UK Government and SMEs supplying government services. The sovereign / air-gapped deployment route (per Principle 21 in `ARC-000-PRIN-v2.0.md`) is **out of scope** of this project and will be tracked under a separate project.
